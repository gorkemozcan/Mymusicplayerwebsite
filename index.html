G Music Entertainment

G Music Entertainment was founded in 1962 by Herb Alpert and Jerry Moss. The first name they chose for their company was Carnival Records. Later they named the company with the initials of A&M, namely Alpert and Moss. A&M was acquired by PolyGram in 1989. In 1998 PolyGram was acquired by Seagrams. Seagrams is part of Universal Music Group. From these mergers, the group called Interscope-Geffen-A&M (IGA) was born. In 2007, with the organization of Universal Music Group, Octone Records ie A&M was reconstructed and albums were made under the label of A&M/Octone Records.
